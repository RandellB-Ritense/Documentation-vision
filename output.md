### Logging into Valtimo

To access Valtimo, you will need to log in using your Microsoft AD credentials. Follow these steps:

1. **Log In**: Click your Microsoft AD account to log in.

### Navigating to Aggregated Data Profiles

Once logged in, follow these steps to manage aggregated data profiles:

1. **Access Profiles**: From the menu, click on "Aggregated Data Profiles."

2. **Select a Profile**: Choose the "demo" profile.

### Managing Caching and Time to Live

To manage caching and time to live settings for the profile, follow these steps:

1. **Enable Caching**: Click on "Caching Enable On" to turn on the caching functionality.

2. **Adjust Time to Live**: Click the minus button to decrease the time to live value or the plus button to increase it.

These steps will allow you to effectively manage caching and time to live settings for aggregated data profiles in Valtimo. If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please contact your system administrator.
