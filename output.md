### Final Version: User Guide for Accessing and Configuring Aggregated Data Profiles

#### Step 1: Login Using Microsoft AD
1. **Initial Login Screen**:
   - Navigate to the login page for "Valtimo (test)".
   - Enter your "Username" and "Password".
   - Choose the "Microsoft AD Ritense" login option.
   - Click on the "LOG IN" button.

2. **Microsoft Account Selection**:
   - You will be redirected to a Microsoft login page.
   - Select your account from the list of available accounts.
   - Click on your account to proceed.

#### Step 2: Accessing Aggregated Data Profiles
1. **Navigation to Aggregated Data Profiles**:
   - After logging in, you will be redirected to the "IKO Admin" application.
   - From the menu, select "Aggregated Data Profiles".

#### Step 3: Selecting and Configuring the Demo Profile
1. **Selecting the Demo Profile**:
   - In the "Aggregated Data Profiles" section, locate and select the "demo" profile.

2. **Configuring Cache Settings**:
   - Navigate to the "General" tab of the demo profile.
   - Enable caching by toggling the "Cache enabled" switch to "On".
   - Adjust the "Time to live" (TTL) value by clicking the minus button to decrease or the plus button to increase the value.
   - The TTL value is set in milliseconds. For example, 600,000 milliseconds equals 10 minutes.

#### Step 4: Managing Relations
1. **Adding a Child Relation**:
   - Navigate to the "Relations" tab.
   - Click the "Add child" button to add a new relation.
   - Fill in the required fields for the new relation, including "From," "Connector instance," "Connector endpoint," "Endpoint parameter Mapping," "Property Name," and "Transform".
   - Configure the cache settings for the new relation by enabling caching and adjusting the TTL value as needed.

#### Step 5: Saving Changes
1. **Saving Configuration**:
   - After making the necessary changes, click the "Save" button to save your configuration.

This guide provides a step-by-step process for logging in using Microsoft AD, accessing the Aggregated Data Profiles section, selecting and configuring the demo profile, managing relations, and saving the changes.
